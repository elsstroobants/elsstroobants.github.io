About me Over mij

I enjoy making things that work well.

With a master's degree in architecture and engineering in my pocket and over a decade of experience in the corporate world, I felt the need to broaden my horizon. I looked for something challenging, fun and meaningful. So, I learned how to code. With my newly acquired skills I am keen to create value for people with good ideas.
